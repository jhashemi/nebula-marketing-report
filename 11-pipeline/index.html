Chapter 06 · Pricing & Pipeline

Four prices,
one engine.

Same model, four packages. The cost-to-serve is identical at the margin — we charge for decision-weight, not compute.

The price ladder is deliberately steep. The $299 Starter is a try-it-before-you-trust-it SKU; we expect 45% of total report volume here, generating roughly 9% of revenue. The Pro tier at $3,500 is the gravity well — it is priced against the manual claim-chart procurement benchmark of $80k–$220k and represents 20% of volume but 49% of revenue. Enterprise at $9,500/month is the exit-the-procurement-loop SKU for the four largest litigation funders; we expect 5–10 of these by end of year one.
