Forecast — Year 2 + Year 3 outlook

The Year-1 plan ends with $207 active customers and an $296k ARR run rate. Year-2 modeling assumes:

  • New-logo acquisition growth slows from 14× (Y1) to 2.5× (Y2) as the easy wins are taken.
  • Net retention rises to 105% (expansion + price increases > gross churn).
  • Paid CAC rises ~30% as we exhaust intent inventory; offset by referral + content compounding.
  • Break-even achieved month 18–22 depending on Series-A timing.
