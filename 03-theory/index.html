Why this works now

The technical preconditions arrived in the last 18 months, not the last 18 years. Foundation-model retrieval on long-context legal text became reliable in 2024. Standards bodies — 3GPP in particular — published machine-readable XML of every technical specification through Release 18 the same year. The combination is what makes the 24-hour read possible. A 2022 attempt at this product would have produced a glorified search engine. A 2026 attempt produces a defensible artifact.

The market preconditions are equally important. WSOU's docket is now public-record proof that the file-first-read-later strategy doesn't pay. Three of the largest litigation funders — Burford, Longford, Omni Bridgeway — have publicly tightened underwriting standards on SEP cases since Q3 2024. The buyers are asking for the read. They have not been served by the incumbents.

Nebula is the read.
That is the entire pitch.
