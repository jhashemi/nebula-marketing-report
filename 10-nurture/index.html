Chapter 10 · Omnichannel Lead Nurture

The 90 days
after the click.

A lead is not a customer. The IP-litigation buyer takes 60–120 days from first touch to signed contract. During that window we must show up everywhere they are — email, LinkedIn, retargeting, podcast, sales calls, physical mail — saying the same thing in their voice, on their schedule. This chapter is the complete plan for the time after the click.

The two nurture tracks

We run two parallel lead nurture programs because a Chief IP Counsel's email cadence and a Senior Litigator's email cadence cannot be the same.

Track 1 · Executive — Chief IP Counsel / VP IP

These buyers do not read transactional sales emails. They read industry briefings from peers. The cadence is quarterly, the format is letter-from-the-founder, the medium is email + physical mail to the office address.

Voice consistency — the same story, 12 places

Across all 12 surfaces above, the message must say the same thing in the buyer's own words. We standardize three core narrative modules and let every channel pick from them:

  1. The WSOU narrative. 205 lawsuits, $635M sought, $0 recovered. Told as a story, not a stat.
  2. The Korean prior art. Concrete artifact (1998 KIPO publication) that PatSnap missed. Specific. Visual. Memorable.
  3. The 21-day deadline. Initial invalidity contentions are due fast. We deliver in 24 hours.

Every email subject line, every ad headline, every LinkedIn post, every podcast intro, every dinner conversation pulls from one of these three modules. The buyer hears the same story across every surface, in slightly different words, until it is the story they tell themselves.

Win-back and recycling

Closed-Lost is not the end. 28% of B2B legal-tech deals close on a second or third attempt, often 6–18 months after first touch. Every lead that goes cold or is closed-lost re-enters a quarterly win-back track:

  1. +30 days: personal email from AE, “I noticed [trigger event] — does this change anything?”
  2. +90 days: case study from a similar account that recently signed.
  3. +180 days: founder LinkedIn DM with a relevant article they will care about.
  4. +365 days: personal invite to a private dinner at the next IPBC / AIPLA / IAM event.
